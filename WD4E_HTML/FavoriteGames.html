Here is a small list of some of my personal favorite video games.

   

Hollow Knight

Hollow Knigth is a 2017 metroidvania vedio game where the player controls a nameless insectlike being and explores the Hollownest, a ruined kingdom full of secrets and fearsome challenges. One of the reasons i love Hollow Knight is because of how much of the Hollownest there is to be explored. The bosses are tough but fair as long as you have their attack patterns down (even then if you're like me that won't matter cause you still suck and need to git gud.) Hollow Knight is a fantastic game once you get past the Forgotten Crossroads, is full of so much lore, and was indeed a truely memorable experiance.

 

Legend of Zelda Tears of the Kingdom

Legend of Zelda Tears of the Kingdom is a game that was released in 2023 that sets you in Hyrule after the events of Breath of the Wild.In this story you by acciedent discover an ancient slumbering evil that is released unto the kingdom and you manage to loss the Princess Zelda. One of the reasons I like totk so much was the story and feeling of adventure I had while trying to track down the missing princess and discovering the souce of the ancient evil. Another thing I really enjoyed was the creation aspect of the game. In totk you have a grand amount of freedom and can build almost anything for any occasion, whether that be creating air planes or korok torture devices.

 

Zenless Zone Zero

Zenless Zone Zero is a free to play gatcha game released in 2024 that has a pretty good fast combat system, good characters, and decent story.Set in New Eridu you play as proxy named Phaeton looking into the disappearance of they're former teacher. along the way you'll discover the secrets of the hollows, meet new compainions, and battle fearsome ethereals.
